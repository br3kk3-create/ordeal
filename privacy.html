Overview

Ordeal is built by DigiWirx LLC. This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind. What happens in your workout stays on your device.

All workout data — including history, custom AMRAPs, and personal stats — is stored locally on your device only. No data is ever sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

No Tracking

Ordeal contains no analytics, no advertising networks, no third-party SDKs, and no tracking of any kind. We have no idea who you are, and that's intentional.

No Account Required

Ordeal requires no account, login, email address, or personal information to use. Download it and go.

Children's Privacy

Ordeal does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13. The app complies fully with COPPA and GDPR requirements.
